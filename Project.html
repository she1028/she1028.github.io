Graphic Design Projects
Turning imagination into striking graphics for digital and print media.

This poster was inspired by the movie “Get Out”, a psychological horror film directed by Jordan Peele and starring Daniel Kaluuya, Allison Williams, and Bradley Whitford.

  • The design reflects the film’s chilling themes of control and identity, showing how people can be manipulated or trapped by hidden forces.
  • The dark tones and haunting imagery of reaching hands symbolize entrapment, fear, and loss of freedom.
  • The red and white typography creates a strong contrast that emphasizes urgency, danger, and emotional tension.
  • The overall layout captures the social commentary and suspenseful atmosphere that made the movie so impactful.
  • The hands overlapping the face create a sense of claustrophobia and loss of autonomy, mirroring the protagonist’s internal conflict.

A wildlife photograph of an eagle using double exposure and shallow depth of field, keeping the eagle sharp while blurring the background for dramatic focus.
